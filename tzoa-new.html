TZOA

A wearable air quality tracker that lets you see the invisible world around you by connecting to your smartphone.

My Role

I was the team lead in charge of organizing development team and integrating individual components into a final prototype. I facilitated weekly meetings and reminded developers of deadlines and happily provided assistance if a task was proving to be more challenging than anticipated. Having a positive team environment was a high priority to me as team lead. I approached all situations with empathy, and worked with all team members to isolate any issues and tackle them as a team.
